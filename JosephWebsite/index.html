A School of Hope

Joseph Academy serves children and adolescents with behavioral, emotional and learning disorders by helping them develop the social, academic and vocational skills they need to function successfully in society.

With the motto of Grow, Learn, Change Joseph Academy provides an environment that is both nurturing and challenging. It seeks to build students’ self-esteem and productivity through a therapeutic program in which staff members constantly express respect and affection for them.

The Goal? To transform these young people into achievers who can effectively reenter the mainstream.

History

We help children and adolescence develop the social, academic and vocational skills they need to function successfully in society. Our goal is to transform these young people into achievers who can effectively reenter the mainstream.

Michael Schack’s passion for helping children is what prompted him to sell his home to open Joseph Academy over 30 years ago. He and his staff are dedicated to recognizing the value of young people who often come from troubled environments or who have been marginalized or neglected most of their lives. The Joseph Academy staff are trained to "catch kids being good" and affirm their self worth through constant interaction. Joseph Academy staff include special education teachers, social workers, teaching assistants, vocational education personnel, a program consultant, psychologist, nurse and physical education teachers.
